WE ARE TRADE FARE

Tradesfare is a Trading and Real Estate management company in New Zealand that was introduced by the government of New Zealand to avoid ponzi schemes, fraud, money laundering and scams (using the social media to misguide individuals). After the last world summit and review of scam records in the year 2001, New Zealand witnessed a decline in scam rate, as a result it became expedient to keep it that way, this is why Jacinda Kate Arden subsequently introduced a law for the elucidation of companies not under the registration of the government of New Zealand. This led to the emergence of Tradesfare, and its introduction as a trading and real estate incorporation with certificate number 6260132 and NZBN 9429046043191
